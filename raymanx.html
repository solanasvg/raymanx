Rayman 3: Hoodlum Havoc

In Rayman 3: Hoodlum Havoc, the story begins when a dark lum named André, born from a Red Lum that became corrupted by nightmare energy, starts transforming other Red Lums into Dark Lums. These Dark Lums then steal fur from animals to create disguises for themselves, becoming the Hoodlums.

André's ultimate goal is to take over the world by multiplying his army of Hoodlums. Rayman's friend Globox accidentally swallows André, leading to a wild chase across the land as they seek Doctor Otto Psi, Romeo Patti, and Art Rytus, the three Teensie doctors who might be able to remove André from Globox's stomach.

Throughout their adventure, Rayman must protect Globox while fighting against the Hoodlum army. He gains access to special powers through "Laser-Washing Powder" cans, which give him temporary abilities like the Heavy Metal Fist, the Lockjaw, and the Shock Rocket. The journey takes them through diverse landscapes, from the Fairy Council to the Desert of the Knaaren, culminating in a final confrontation with André at the Tower of the Leptys.

Main Characters

Rayman

The main hero of the series, Rayman is a limbless character with floating hands, feet, and head. Despite his unusual appearance, he's brave, determined, and possesses incredible agility. He has the power to throw energy-charged fists, glide using his hair like a helicopter, and gain various special abilities throughout his adventures.

Globox

Rayman's best friend, Globox is a large, blue, good-natured creature who often finds himself in trouble. In Rayman 3, he accidentally swallows André, setting the main plot in motion. Despite his cowardly nature, he's loyal to Rayman and provides both comic relief and crucial help throughout their adventures.

The Teensie King

Leader of the Teensies, small magical creatures who guard the world's portals. The Teensie King, along with his subjects, helps Rayman navigate through different worlds. Despite their small size, Teensies possess powerful magic and knowledge of the world's secrets.

André

The main antagonist of Rayman 3, André is a Dark Lum who was once a Red Lum before being corrupted by nightmare energy. He leads the Hoodlum army and aims to take over the world. His transformation from a Red Lum represents the game's themes of corruption and redemption.

Reflux

A powerful Knaaren warrior who serves as one of the main antagonists in Rayman 3. After being defeated by Rayman in the Desert of the Knaaren, he seeks revenge and eventually becomes André's strongest ally. His immense strength and pride make him a formidable opponent.
